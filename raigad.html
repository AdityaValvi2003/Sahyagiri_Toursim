Historical Significance:
Raigad Fort was the capital of the Maratha Empire after Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj’s coronation in 1674. It stands as a symbol of Maratha pride and resilience, offering breathtaking views and rich history.


Attractions:
  • Maha Darwaja
  • Raigad Ropeway
  • Samadhi of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj
  • Jagdishwar Temple
  • Hirkani Buru

Best Time to Visit: October to March (winter for clear views, monsoon for lush greenery).

Ideal for: History enthusiasts, adventure lovers, photographers, and nature lovers.
